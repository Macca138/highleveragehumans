## Design Guidelines for Premium Domain

- Primary: #1a1a1a (Dark theme)
- Secondary: #ffffff (Light contrast)
- Accent: #0066cc (Premium blue)
- Success: #00cc66 (Profit green)

### Symbol Options
1. **HLH Monogram** - Clean, professional lettermark
2. **Leverage Symbol** - Abstract arrow/lever icon
3. **Growth Chart** - Upward trending line/bar
4. **Diamond/Premium** - Luxury positioning symbol

### Design Requirements
- High contrast for visibility at small sizes
- Premium/professional appearance
- Scalable vector-based design
- Consistent across all formats
- Recognizable at 16x16 pixels
